    About Me

    Born and raised in Atlanta, GA, Messenger is an artist and producer who has been creating for five years. Ever since his debut EP Misguided, he has sought to find meaning in his work. With influences from legends such as Stevie Wonder, Notorious B.I.G. and Nas to newer acts like Don Toliver and JID, he creates a sound that is unique and visionary.
